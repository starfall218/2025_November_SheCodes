The Work Song — A Lesson in Kindness

Cinderella lived with her stepmother and stepsisters who treated her cruelly and left her to do all the housework. Yet, she remained kind and gentle. Her good heart didn't go unnoticed — the mice and birds saw her kindness and decided to help. In the "Work Song," they stitched her dress together, showing that when you're good to others, help can come from the most unexpected places.
